Privacy Policy

SeenHome · 看见家 · 見えない家事ノート

App: SeenHome (看见家 / 見えない家事ノート)
Developer: sens9012
Contact: liyunkang1996@gmail.com

Summary

SeenHome is a local-first, offline app for recording invisible household work. It has no account system, no ads, and no cloud sync. SeenHome does not collect, upload, or share any of your personal data. Everything you create stays on your own device.

Data We Collect

None. SeenHome does not collect personal data and does not send it anywhere. There is no user account, no sign-in, no analytics account, and no advertising SDK that transmits your information off the device.

Data Stored on Your Device

The following is created and stored only on your device, and is never transmitted to us or to any third-party server:

  • Household work records (labor records)
  • Photos attached to records
  • Schedules / to-do items (the "arrange" module)
  • Frequently-used targets and person names you enter
  • Categories, value rules, and app settings (language, reminders)
  • Backup files you export

This data lives in SeenHome's local storage on your device. If you uninstall the app or clear its data, this information is removed from the device (except for backup files you have already exported elsewhere).

Photos

When you attach a photo to a record, SeenHome saves an internal copy inside the app. Deleting a record in SeenHome only removes SeenHome's internal copy — it does not delete the original photo in your phone's photo gallery. Photos are never uploaded to any server.

Backups

Backups are exported manually by you as encrypted files (.shb). SeenHome does not store your backup password, and it is never sent anywhere. If you forget the backup password, the backup cannot be recovered. You are responsible for keeping your exported backup files and their passwords safe.

Local Network (Wi-Fi) Import

SeenHome offers an optional import feature over your local network. When you start it, the app runs a temporary local upload page so you can send a backup file from a computer on the same Wi-Fi network. This transfer happens only within your local network and only while you actively start it. No files are uploaded to any external server, and the temporary page stops after the transfer completes or times out.

Reports and Sharing

Report images and schedule images are generated on your device. They are only shared if you actively choose to share them through your device's standard system share sheet. SeenHome does not automatically send or publish anything on your behalf.

Notifications

SeenHome uses local notifications only, to remind you about the start or end of your schedules. Notifications are scheduled on the device and do not depend on any network or push service.

Children's Privacy

SeenHome is a general-purpose personal record-keeping tool and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy
